Societal Mission

The mission of Air University is to achieve excellence in teaching and research for producing graduates with sound professional knowledge,integrity of character, a keen sense of social responsibility and a passion for lifelong learning.The Air University in Islamabad, Pakistan, is dedicated to fulfilling a multifaceted mission centered around education, research, and national defense. At its core, the university is committed to providing high-quality education and training in the realm of aviation, aeronautics, and related disciplines. Offering a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, the institution aims to equip its students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the dynamic field of aerospace.
